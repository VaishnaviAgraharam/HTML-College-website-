G Pullaiah College of Engineering and Technology

Accredited by NAAC with A Grade CIV, CSE, ECE & EEE NBA Accredited approved by AICTE and Affiliated to JNTUA

G Pullaiah College of Engineering and Technology is a college situated in Pasupula, just outside Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh, India. It was established in 2007 by G. Pullaiah. The college is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education and affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Anantapur.
